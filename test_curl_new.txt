
RESOURCES
=========

/<patientID>/login?first_name=<first_name>&last_name=<last_name> (GET)
/symptomslist (GET)
/<patientID>/condition (multipart POST)
/practitioner?first_name=<first_name>&last_name=<last_name> (GET)
/<patientID>/communication/<practitionerID> (multipart POST)
